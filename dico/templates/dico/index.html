    Welcome to I Do Declare

    I Do Declare is dedicated to helping all of us understand important issues and to voice our opinions on the matters before the legislators who are responsible for representing us in the U.S. Congress.

    Here you can:

    • create actions that should be taken up by Congress;
    • discover actions others have created;
    • support or oppose any action;
    • give your reasons for supporting or opposing an action;
    • rank the reasons that other people give.

    We look forward to cross-partisan conversations where we can come together to appreciate different points of view and better understand why we hold the positions that we do.

    Getting Started

    Sign in to identify the issues you care about and vote on actions Congress can take. Pick an issue you care about, vote on an action associated with that issue, or create a new action that you think our Congress should take. Give your reasons. Feel good about participating in our Democracy.

    Don't have an account? Create one now.

    Send feedback to feedback@idodeclare.org.
