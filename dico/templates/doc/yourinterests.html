Your Interests

Expressing interest in an issue connects you to a community of citizens who share your interest.

Once you express interest in an issue, you can create actions associated with that issue and vote on any action associated with your interest. You can add or remove an issue from your list of interested issues at any time.

If you have a suggestion for an issue that is not available, send us your suggestion to feedback@idodeclare.org and we will take a look.
